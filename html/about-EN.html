Who am I?

Hi! I'm Moon, a designer in training obsessed with transforming ideas into memorable visual experiences.

Currently studying Digital Design at PUC-SP, I am originally from Bolivia and came to Brazil seeking new knowledge and opportunities to grow in the design field.

My journey in design started with curiosity and grew into a true passion. I love exploring new techniques, tools, and trends that help me create more innovative and impactful projects.

This month I also rewatched True Beauty, a k-drama. This emotional re-reading led me to create a photopoem: a mix between poem and images, something I learned from a teacher in Bolivia and that I love.

About the photopoem:

It's a technique I learned in Bolivia: combining poetry with images in a way that one complements the other. It's not just putting text over a photo, it's creating a visual-poetic dialogue.

Around the same time, in Brazil the "love strawberry" became trendy: strawberry wrapped in white brigadeiro and covered with caramel. I never got to try it, but it looked amazing.
